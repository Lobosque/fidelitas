# Briefing — Voltei Logo

## About the product

**Voltei** (Portuguese for "I came back") is a digital loyalty platform for small and medium businesses (barbershops, coffee shops, restaurants, etc.). Customers scan a QR code at the store, get a loyalty card straight in their Google/Apple Wallet, and accumulate check-ins until they earn a reward. No app to download.

The name "Voltei" comes from the concept of **return** — the customer who comes back, loyalty, the cycle.

## What I need

An **icon/symbol** that works on its own (without text), but also pairs well with the name "Voltei" next to it when needed.

### Where the logo will be used

- **Web app** (navbar, favicon)
- **Google/Apple Wallet card** (small icon ~80px)
- **Printed totem** with QR code (on paper, next to the brand name)
- **Light and dark backgrounds** (must work on both)

The logo must be legible at very small sizes (favicon 32px, wallet icon 80px).

## Visual style

- **Modern and tech** — clean, geometric, minimalist
- **Fun and colorful** — energetic, youthful, dynamic
- The balance between these two is key: it shouldn't feel too corporate, nor too childish

## Concepts to explore

- **Return / coming back** — circular arrows, loops, cycles
- **Check-in / progress** — completion marks, filled dots
- **Digital wallet** — subtle reference to wallet/card
- **Letter "V"** — the brand initial, can be stylized

## Colors

- **Fully open** — feel free to suggest any palette
- Should be vibrant and memorable
- Must work in monochrome (black or white) for limited use cases

## What I do NOT want

- Generic "loyalty" logos with stars and crowns
- Too many details that won't work at small sizes
- Complex gradients that don't print well
- Corporate/enterprise look — the audience is barbershop and café owners, not Fortune 500

## Delivery format

- Vector SVG (preferred)
- Versions: full color, monochrome white, monochrome black
- Transparent background
